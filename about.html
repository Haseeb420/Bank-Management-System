About Bank

Bank Managent System

Bank Management System will use for managing accounts, transactions and customer details. There will be a manager who will approve loan for customer and also manage accountant. Accountant will have Id, name, contact number and will interact with system by entering valid password. His ID will work as his username for login. Manager can also interact with system through his Id and password, moreover manager has name and contact number. Customer can eligible to interact with system only when he/she has account in bank. Each account has ID, name and contain amount. Customer login to his/her account using his/her id allotting by the bank and password. Through which he/she can transfer amount and can see details of bank account and he/she can update his/her password. Customer may or may not apply for loan. If customer apply for loan it will approve by the manager under terms and conditions and desirable time period. Loan will return back in the form of installments. There is a super admin in Bank Managment System who will manage all Banks under his/her area included Managers, Customers, Employees, Accounts and all other things related to different Banks Branches.
